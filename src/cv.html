E.L.S.A.
Emergency Landing Site Assessment
On the quest to make general aviation safer, I collaborated with my flight instructor to develop a tool which assists pilots in assessing the risk involved with overflying populated areas at low altitudes — as is sometimes required by airspace restrictions.
https://elsa.tibl.dev
OrbCast
Satellite Orbit Prediction Toolkit
In order to make satellite tracking more accessible for RF enthusiasts, I brought a state-of-the-art NASA framework to the browser. This allows users to accurately predict when an object of interest passes and thus plan communication more easily from any device!
https://orbcast.tibl.dev
